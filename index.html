About the UGC NET Exam

UGC NET (National Eligibility Test) is conducted to determine the eligibility for Assistant Professorship and Junior Research Fellowship in Indian universities and colleges.

This site is dedicated to the Computer Science and Applications paper. It includes:

  • Paper 1 - Common for all, testing Teaching and Research Aptitude.
  • Paper 2 - Subject-specific: Computer Science and Applications.

The exam is moderately difficult and demands clarity in fundamentals, concept linkage, and frequent revision.
